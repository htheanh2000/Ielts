Phần I · Lý thuyếtHai loại process

Two Process Types · Linear vs Cyclical

Hai dạng — hai cách mở bài.

Type 01 — Manufacturing

Linear

  • Đặc điểm: bắt đầu input cụ thể → kết thúc output cụ thể
  • Ví dụ: chocolate production · brick-making · paper recycling · cement & concrete
  • Thì: present simple passive (is heated · are collected)
  • Mở bài: "The diagram illustrates how X is produced / manufactured."
  • Sequencers: First · Once X-ed · Subsequently · Finally

Type 02 — Natural / Biological

Cyclical

  • Đặc điểm: vòng khép kín, không có điểm kết — output = input của vòng kế tiếp
  • Ví dụ: water cycle · carbon cycle · life cycle of salmon / butterfly / frog
  • Thì: present simple (active or passive — biological action)
  • Mở bài: "The diagram depicts the cycle of X / the life cycle of X."
  • Sequencers: Once X-ed · Subsequently · The cycle then begins again

Cyclical luôn cần câu cuối: "the cycle then repeats / begins again" — chốt vòng tuần hoàn.

Phần I · Lý thuyếtSequencers

Sequencers · Five-stage chain

Sequencers — trải đều, không lặp.

01 · Start
First / To begin / Initially
Mở đầu bài. Không bao giờ dùng "Firstly" và "Secondly" cùng nhau.
02 · Next
Once X-ed / Having been X-ed
Câu nối ưa thích band 6.5+. Once heated · Having been mixed.
03 · Then
Subsequently / Thereafter
Thay then. Đặt đầu câu, theo dấu phẩy.
04 · After
After this / Following this stage
Dùng giữa Body 1 / Body 2. Linking đoạn lớn.
05 · End / Loop
Finally / The cycle then repeats
Linear: Finally. Cyclical: begins again.

Quy tắc: trong 1 đoạn không dùng quá 2 lần cùng 1 sequencer. Trải đều 5 nhóm trên → Coherence & Cohesion lên 1 band.

Phần I · Lý thuyếtPassive voice

Passive Voice · Three patterns

Passive — vì người làm không quan trọng.

PATTERN 01
Present simple passive
is/are + V3

The beans are roasted at 120°C.
The mixture is poured into moulds.
PATTERN 02
Once + past participle
Once + V3, ...

Once roasted, the beans are cracked open.
Once heated, the metal is shaped.
PATTERN 03
Having been + V3
Having been V3, ...

Having been crushed, the cocoa is mixed with sugar.
Having been filtered, the water flows downward.

Đừng lặp is/are V3 8 lần. Luân phiên 3 pattern trên → GRA tăng ngay 1 band.

Writing Task 1 · Academic07 / 50
Phần I · Lý thuyếtNgôn ngữ vòng tuần hoàn

Cyclical Language · Loop & recurrence

Vòng — không có start, không có end.

Mở bài cyclical

Opening verbs

  • Begins: The cycle begins with X / commences when …
  • Starts: X marks the beginning of the cycle
  • Note: "begin" — không có "the start" tuyệt đối, chỉ là điểm tiện để mô tả
  • Vào câu mở: "Although the cycle has no fixed starting point, it is conventional to begin with X."

Đóng vòng cyclical

Closing verbs

  • Repeats: the cycle then repeats / repeats itself
  • Begins again: the cycle begins again / starts anew
  • Returns: the process returns to the original stage
  • Recurs: the cycle recurs indefinitely / continues

Câu cuối bài cyclical bắt buộc có verb "lặp lại". Thiếu = bài thiếu kết → trừ Task Achievement.

Step 3 · Body paragraphs · 9 phút

Hai đoạn — cultivation 1-3 / processing 4-7.

Body 1 · CultivationThe process begins with the harvesting of cocoa pods, which are picked directly from the trees. Once harvested, the beans inside are fermented for between five and seven days, after which they are spread out on mats and dried in the sun. This first phase is purely agricultural, requiring no industrial equipment.

Body 2 · ProcessingSubsequently, the dried beans are roasted in ovens at around 120°C, then cracked open and ground into a thick paste known as cocoa liquor. After this stage, the liquor is combined with sugar, milk and cocoa butter to form a smooth mixture. Finally, the mixture is poured into moulds and left to cool, producing the chocolate bars that reach consumers.

Sequencers trải đều: begins with · Once harvested · after which · Subsequently · After this stage · Finally — sáu kết nối khác nhau.

Language analysis · Linear process

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo từ bài chocolate.

  • Passive variety"are picked · are fermented · are spread out and dried · are roasted · are cracked open · is combined with · is poured · is left to cool" — tám passive khác nhau, không lặp.
  • Sequencers"begins with · Once harvested · after which · Subsequently · After this stage · Finally" — sáu sequencer khác nhau, trải đều 2 body.
  • Reduced clauses"requiring no industrial equipment · producing the chocolate bars · known as cocoa liquor" — participle clauses để gắn lý do/kết quả → GRA.
  • Lexical precision"harvested · cultivation · spread out · cocoa liquor · smooth mixture · moulding" — vocabulary chuyên ngành chế biến, không dùng made · put · finished.

"Once harvested" là cấu trúc nâng band: thay after they are harvested bằng 2 từ → câu gọn hơn.

Step 3 · Body paragraphs · 9 phút

Hai đoạn — upward / downward.

Body 1 · UpwardIt is conventional to begin with the sea, where solar energy causes water to evaporate from the surface and rise into the atmosphere as vapour. Once it reaches a sufficient altitude, the vapour cools and condenses, forming the dense clouds that gather above both land and sea. This first half of the cycle therefore moves water from the surface upward into the sky.

Body 2 · Downward · LoopSubsequently, the moisture stored in the clouds is released as precipitation in the form of rain or snow, much of which falls onto the land. At this point, the cycle begins again, repeating indefinitely.

Bài mẫu — cyclical đầy đủ.

IntroThe diagram depicts the water cycle, the natural and continuous process by which water circulates between the oceans, the atmosphere and the land.

OverviewOverall, it is clear that the cycle consists of four key stages — evaporation, condensation, precipitation and collection — driven primarily by the energy of the sun. As the cycle has no fixed beginning or end, water continuously circulates between the sea, the sky and the land.

Body 1It is conventional to begin with the sea, where solar energy causes water to evaporate from the surface and rise into the atmosphere as vapour. Once it reaches a sufficient altitude, the vapour cools and condenses, forming the dense clouds that gather above both land and sea. This first half of the cycle therefore moves water from the surface upward into the sky.

Body 2Subsequently, the moisture stored in the clouds is released as precipitation in the form of rain or snow, much of which falls onto the land. From there, water either flows over the surface as runoff or seeps underground as groundwater, and in both cases it eventually returns to the sea. At this point, the cycle begins again, repeating indefinitely.

Practice 02 · Water CyclePhân tích ngôn ngữ

Language analysis · Cyclical process

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo từ bài Water Cycle.

  • Cyclical phrases"continuously circulates · has no fixed beginning or end · the cycle begins again · repeating indefinitely" — bốn cụm chuyên cho cyclical, không thấy ở linear.
  • Verb variety"evaporate · rise · cools and condenses · is released as · flows · seeps · returns" — bảy verb mô tả pha thay đổi vật lý.
  • Reduced clauses"forming the dense clouds · much of which falls · stored in the clouds · repeating indefinitely" — relative clauses + participle clauses → câu phức tự nhiên.
  • Either-or structure"either flows over the surface as runoff or seeps underground as groundwater" — cấu trúc song song để mô tả 2 nhánh.

"It is conventional to begin with…" — câu mở Body 1 đặc trưng cho cyclical. Học thuộc.

Body 1 · FreshwaterThe cycle begins when eggs are laid in the gravel of a riverbed, where they remain protected for between five and six months before hatching. Language analysis · Life cycle

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo từ bài Salmon.

  • Cyclical phrases"the cycle begins · then begins again with a new generation · spans roughly six years · the very river where they were born" — bốn cụm vòng tuần hoàn + relative clause.
