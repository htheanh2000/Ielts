Two Map Types · Tense decisions

Hai dạng — hai thì khác nhau.

Type 01 — Most common

Past ↔ Present

  • Tín hiệu: năm cụ thể quá khứ (1990 vs 2020 / in the past vs today)
  • Thì: past simple passive — was built / was demolished / was replaced by
  • Verb: was knocked down · was converted into · was extended · was constructed
  • Linking: in the meantime · over this period · by 2020

Type 02 — Less common

Present ↔ Future

  • Tín hiệu: "proposed plan" / "future development" / "is to be" / "planned changes"
  • Thì: future passive — will be built / is going to be / is to be constructed
  • Verb: will be demolished · is set to · is planned to · will be replaced by
  • Linking: in the future · according to the proposal · once completed

Sai thì = sai cả bài. Đọc caption trước khi viết một chữ. Tìm năm hoặc từ "proposed".

Phần I · Lý thuyếtNgôn ngữ thay đổi · Quá khứ

Change Language · Past Simple Passive

Past passive — was + V3.

Removal · Replacement

Mất đi / Thay thế

  • Demolish: was demolished / was knocked down / was pulled down
  • Remove: was removed / was cleared / was relocated
  • Replace: was replaced by / was converted into / gave way to
  • Transform: was turned into / was redeveloped as

Addition · Extension

Mới / Mở rộng

  • Build: was built / was constructed / was erected
  • Add: was added / was introduced / was installed
  • Extend: was extended / was expanded / was enlarged
  • Plant: was planted / was landscaped / was added with greenery

Công thức: [Subject] was/were [V3] [by-phrase optional]. Ví dụ: The old library was replaced by a shopping centre.

Phần I · Lý thuyếtNgôn ngữ thay đổi · Tương lai

Change Language · Future Passive

Future passive — will be + V3.

Three structures

Cấu trúc

  • Will-passive: will be built / will be demolished
  • Going-to: is going to be replaced / are going to be relocated
  • Be-to: is to be constructed / is to be added (formal — đề proposal)
  • Set / planned: is set to · is planned to · is scheduled to

Adverbs of certainty

Mức độ chắc chắn

  • Definite: will definitely · is certain to be
  • Likely: is expected to · is likely to · is anticipated to
  • Possible: may be · could be · is being considered
  • Order: first · subsequently · once completed · finally

Map proposal thường dùng "is to be" — formal, ngắn gọn. Ví dụ: A new marina is to be built on the southern shore.

Four-paragraph structure · Maps

Bốn đoạn cố định — grouping mới là chìa khoá.

~25w
PARA 01
Introduction
Paraphrase câu đề. Đề cập 2 thời điểm + địa điểm. The two maps illustrate / depict / show how X changed between A and B.
~40w
PARA 02
Overview
2 câu · 2 đặc trưng tổng thể: hướng phát triển (commercialised / urbanised / modernised) + cái gì giữ nguyên.
~45w
PARA 03
Body 1
Nhóm 1 thay đổi: theo khu vực (e.g. changes in the north) hoặc theo loại (e.g. new constructions). 3-4 thay đổi cụ thể.
~45w
PARA 04
Body 2
Nhóm 2 còn lại + cái unchanged. Linking: Meanwhile · As for · The southern half · In contrast.

Chia nhóm trước khi viết. Nếu trộn lung tung → mất Coherence & Cohesion. Hai cách: spatial (theo hướng) hoặc thematic (theo loại).

Exam Sequence · 20-minute budget

20 phút — 5 bước cố định.

  1. Phân tích đề + 2 maps (3 phút).Xác định: 2 thời điểm · loại đề (past↔present hay present↔future) · compass · 4-6 thay đổi chính · cái gì giữ nguyên.

  5. Check lỗi (2 phút).Thì động từ (passive đúng V3?) · số ít/nhiều · spatial preposition · đếm từ (≥150). Không viết thêm.

Language analysis · Why this scores Band 6.5

Bốn kỹ thuật ngôn ngữ — tháo ra từ bài mẫu.

  • Paraphrase"shows changes that took place" → "illustrate how … was redeveloped" — đổi shows thành illustrate, dùng was redeveloped để gói gọn ý "thay đổi".
  • Passive variety"was demolished · was extended · was replaced by · was converted into · was added · remained unchanged" — sáu cấu trúc passive khác nhau, không lặp 1 verb 2 lần.
  • Spatial language"to the north of Main Street · on its eastern half · westward · at the eastern end · to the south" — đủ 4 lớp: compass + relative + direction + edge.
  • Cohesion"Furthermore · In contrast · marking a clear shift · providing a sense of continuity" — linking + participle clauses → cohesion mượt, không lặp and.

Có cả 4 kỹ thuật → Band 6.5 chắc chắn. Thiếu 1 → tụt 0.5 band.

Language analysis · Future passive techniques

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo ra từ bài mẫu.

  • Future passive variety"will be transformed · is to be replaced by · will be widened · are set to give way to · will be converted into · will be redeveloped as · will be preserved" — bảy cấu trúc khác nhau, không lặp will be built 5 lần.
  • Spatial language"along the seafront · to the south of this road · further inland · on the western side" — đủ 4 lớp: along + to-the-south-of + further inland + on-the-side.
  • Cohesion"The most prominent change · Furthermore · By contrast · ensuring that …" — linking + participle clause cuối câu để gắn ý "preserved → retained character".
  • Lexical precision"redeveloped · transformed · accommodate · anticipated tourist arrivals · alongside" — từ vựng C1, không dùng change · build · for · with.

Future map nào cũng cần đủ 4 kỹ thuật trên — đặc biệt passive variety vì là kỹ thuật bị giám khảo soi đầu tiên.

Step 3 · Body paragraphs · 9 phút

Spatial grouping — north academic / east services.

Body 1 · North · AcademicThe most extensive changes took place along the northern edge of the campus. The library, originally a modest building in the north-west, was extended eastward, almost doubling in size. To the north-east, the old dormitory was demolished and rebuilt as a much larger student residence. These developments suggest a deliberate effort to accommodate a growing student population.

Body 2 · East · ServicesAlong the eastern edge, the café was replaced by a new science centre, signalling a shift toward research facilities. Adjacent to it, the single-level parking area was rebuilt as a multi-level car park, increasing capacity without expanding the footprint. By contrast, the central main building and the western sports field were left untouched throughout the period.

Bài có 5 changes + 2 unchanged → cần phân nhóm chặt. Spatial north/east dễ viết hơn thematic.

IntroThe two maps illustrate how the campus of Hillside University was transformed over a twenty-five-year period from 2000 to 2025.

Language analysis · Spatial precision

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo từ bài Hillside.

  • Compass + relative"in the north-west · to the north-east · along the eastern edge · adjacent to it · to the west · at the centre" — sáu cách định vị khác nhau.
  • Passive variety"was transformed · was extended · was demolished and rebuilt · was replaced by · was rebuilt as · were left untouched" — sáu cấu trúc khác nhau.
  • Participle clauses"signalling a shift · increasing capacity · accommodate a growing population" — dùng -ing và to-V để gắn lý do/hệ quả → tăng GRA.
  • Lexical variety"considerable · modernisation · modest · doubling in size · deliberate effort · footprint" — không lặp big · changed · effort · area.

"Increasing capacity without expanding the footprint" — câu nâng band: gắn kết quả vào phương tiện qua participle.

Step 3 · Body paragraphs · 9 phút

Spatial — north (community) / south (transport).

Body 1 · NorthTo the north of the central road, the three existing factories are to be demolished. Factory A and Factory B in the western and central blocks will be replaced by two residential developments, while Factory C on the eastern side will be redeveloped as a primary school. This northern half is therefore set to be transformed into a community-oriented zone.

Body 2 · SouthAcross the road, the southern half will see equally drastic change. The warehouses occupying the south-west will be cleared to make way for a public park, while the freight yard in the south-east is to be converted into a light rail station with an adjoining plaza. Meanwhile, the central road itself will be repurposed from truck access to pedestrian use, complementing the new residential character of the estate.

Language analysis · Large-scale change

Bốn kỹ thuật — tháo từ bài Greenfield.

  • Future passive variety"are to be demolished · will be replaced by · will be redeveloped as · is set to be transformed · will be cleared · is to be converted into · will be repurposed" — bảy cấu trúc, không trùng lặp.
  • Spatial language"to the north of · in the western and central blocks · on the eastern side · across the road · in the south-east · meanwhile" — sáu cách định vị.
  • Adverbs of degree"complete shift · equally drastic · community-oriented · entire site" — adverb & adjective mạnh để diễn tả đại quy mô.
  • Cohesion"This northern half is therefore set to · Across the road, the southern half will see · Meanwhile · complementing" — chuyển ý mượt giữa Body 1 ↔ Body 2 mà không lặp However.

"Set to make way for" là cụm collocation idiomatic — cực kỳ ăn điểm cho đề proposed.

Phần VI · Tổng kếtPattern chung

Common pattern · Across all 4 essays

Một khung — bốn bài.

01 · Intro (1 câu)
"The two maps illustrate / depict + how + X + was redeveloped / will be transformed + over [period] / under [scheme]."
02 · Overview (2 câu)
Câu 1: hướng phát triển tổng thể (commercialised / urbanised / regenerated). Câu 2: cái unchanged.
03 · Body 1 (3-4 câu)
Nhóm spatial 1 hoặc thematic 1 (e.g. north / seafront / academic block) — 3-4 thay đổi, mỗi câu 1 verb passive khác.
04 · Body 2 (3-4 câu)
Nhóm còn lại + linking với unchanged ở cuối. Meanwhile · By contrast · Across the road · Further inland.

Cùng khung 4 đoạn — chỉ thay verb passive theo thì (past was vs future will be / is to be). Đổi gì thuộc về nội dung; cấu trúc giữ nguyên.
